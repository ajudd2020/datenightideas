Option 2: This is what you mean to me...

For this date you have to find different objects around your home. But, this isn't like the scavenger hunt dates (if you've done those already)

For this date, you will bring objects that you find throughout the day to dinner or some other time you agree to share what you find with each other.

You should find one object to represent each of the following things:

  • How you felt on your first date with the other person.
  • How you currently feel about the other person.
  • What you are most thankful for about the other person.
  • Your future with the other person.
Let me know what you thought of this date night!

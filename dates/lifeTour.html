Option 3: Life Tour

This date involves traveling YOUR world (wide web)!

The goal? Take your partner on a trip through your life. Use the internet to show them where you grew up, your high school, the mall you hung out at with your friends in high school.

Heck! You could even show them pictures of all the horrible fashion trends you followed when you were young (why-oh-why did I wear two polos at one time?!)

Ideas to include:

  • Where you grew up
  • Somewhere where you have your first memory as a child
  • Somewhere where you have your best memory as a child
  • Somewhere you hung out all the time
  • Other places that are significant to you and who you are

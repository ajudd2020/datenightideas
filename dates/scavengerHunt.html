Option 1: Scavenger Hunt!

For this date you have to find different objects around your home.

You will judge each other based on if you believe the item fulfills the requirements.

The items you have to find (in order) are...

  • Something that perfectly matches your partner's favorite color (we're starting easy!)
  • Something that represents a chore your partner hates to do.
  • Your partner's favorite book or movie.
  • Something in your kitchen your partner refuses (or at least hates) to eat.
  • Something your partner would love to see you wear.
Let me know what you thought of this date night!

Option 1: Scavenger Hunt (Romantic Version)!

For this date you have to find different objects around your home.

You will judge each other based on if you believe the item fulfills the requirements.

The items you have to find (in order) are...

  • A gift you gave the other person for a special event.
  • Something that reminds the other person of when you first started dating.
  • Something that reminds the other person of your first date.
  • Something that reminds the other person of your favorite place to go together (or a favorite vacation).
  • Something the other person has that is actually yours.
Let me know what you thought of this date night!

Option 2: Blindfold Time!

This date involves a blindfold... So, you know, be prepared.

You will have to move around wearing a blindfold. So, BE IN A SAFE PLACE!

No stairs. Nothing sharp. Be smart. You are responsible for the location you pick to complete this date.

Follow my voice (and my directions!)

For this challenge, you are going to see how well you really know your home.

I mean... you've spent the last like 8 years locked up at home, right? Easy Peasy!

To complete this date, both of you must complete the following steps:

  • One of you will chose a location in the home (on the same level--no stairs, remember!) to "hide"
  • You will describe your location to your partner as they (wearing a blindfold) try to find you.
  • In order to "pass" this date, your partner has to make it to where you are! SO HELP THEM! It is up to you to get them to where you are safely!
  • (This is me washing my hands of responsibility. Your partner's safety is in your hands!)
  • The date isn't over until you have both had a turn!
